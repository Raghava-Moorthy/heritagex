USES & BENEFITS



Ashwagandha is perhaps best known for its ability to reduce stress. It’s classified as an adaptogen, a substance that helps the body cope with stress.

The ashwagandha may help reduce symptoms of other mental health conditions, including depression, in certain populations.

The ashwagandha may have some benefits for people with diabetes or high blood sugar levels.

Ashwagandha could improve cognitive functioning in certain populations, including older adults with mild cognitive impairment

Many people take ashwagandha to promote restful sleep, and some evidence suggests it may help with sleep issues.
